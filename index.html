About Me

My name is Elena Ahern, I graduated from High School 1327 (formarly Sir Francis Drake High School) in June of 2019, and am now persuing a BA in Political Science at Univeristy of California, Riverside. My true calling is leadership, beginning this journey as a volleybal coach in highschool, I have found that guiding others and trailblazing my areas of expertise fufills me like nothing else. I am the current Education Chairman for Epsilon Pi chapter of Kappa Kappa Gamma, which responsibilities include educating new members about the history and importance of our chapter, and providing a powerful and immersive experience to all of our members, especially the seniors.

Some of my other passions are ceramics, literature and long distance running. While persuing an art degree was definitley an interest (having won 1st place in my counties art contest) , the decision to follow Political Science felt right. I wanted to choose a career path that would fuel this flame I have towards making the world a better place environmentally, economically and socially.
